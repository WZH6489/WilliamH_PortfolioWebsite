SFU SideQuests

A gamified campus companion for SFU students. Discover sidequests (social, academic, fitness, exploration) during time between classes.

View on Devpost

Inspiration

A common complaint about school life is how monotonous and boring campus life may get. SFU SideQuest was made so that students have activities that they can do between classes as a way to engage with the community, campus and environment around SFU.

What it does

There are challenges, ranging from social, academic, fitness and exploration, all designed for students to engage with their community. There is also a custom "Add Quest" feature to add your own quests.

How we built it

The app/website was made with Figma.

What we learned

We learned how to use Figma, and Git.
